About us

Founded on lessons learned the hard way.

Our story

Talent isn't the problem. Access is.

The inspiration behind IADEBAYO Foundation stems from the entrepreneurial journey of its visionary, Dare Adebayo, who began his entrepreneurial journey immediately after secondary school and launched his second business while at university. Like many first-time entrepreneurs, he experienced numerous setbacks, learned difficult lessons, and navigated the uncertainties of building a business with limited guidance.

It was only after participating in startup accelerator programmes that he gained the practical insights, mentorship, and strategic thinking that transformed his approach to building and growing businesses.

Many promising young entrepreneurs do not fail because they lack ambition or talent; they fail because they lack access to the right knowledge, mentors, and entrepreneurial community at the right time.

Driven by this conviction, Dare founded the Embark Entrepreneurship Academy, the flagship initiative of IADEBAYO Foundation, to equip undergraduate students, recent graduates, and early-stage entrepreneurs across Africa with practical entrepreneurship education, mentorship, and a supportive community.

Our mission is simple: to ensure that the next generation of African entrepreneurs starts with the guidance, community, and opportunities that many founders wish they had from day one.

Vision

To be the foremost organization grooming young people to be entrepreneurs and change-makers.

Mission

Provide a platform to inspire, groom, empower, and mentor young people to acquire skills, become creative, innovative, and enterprising.

What we believe

Five convictions guide everything we do.

  • Entrepreneurship can be taught.
  • Young people should have access to practical entrepreneurial education early.
  • Sustainable businesses create sustainable communities.
  • Mentorship accelerates success.
  • Leadership and character are as important as business skills.
