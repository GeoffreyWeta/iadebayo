Join our faculty — facilitator or mentor

Help develop the entrepreneurs who will shape Africa's future.

We invite accomplished entrepreneurs, business leaders, and distinguished professionals to contribute their expertise as Facilitators and Mentors. By sharing your experience, you will help emerging founders make better decisions, overcome challenges, and build resilient, high-impact businesses.

The programme is delivered entirely online, enabling you to contribute from anywhere in the world. Live teaching sessions are held on weekends to accommodate your work schedule, while one-to-one mentorship sessions are scheduled flexibly based on each mentor's availability.
