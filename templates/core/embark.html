Overview · Why Embark exists

1.3 billion young Africans by 2050. Entrepreneurship has to work.

By 2050, Africa is projected to have a population of approximately 2.5 billion people, with around 1.3 billion between the ages of 15 and 34. Entrepreneurship will play a vital role in creating employment for this rapidly growing youth population.

Yet research consistently shows that many businesses fail because founders lack access to practical business education, experienced mentors, entrepreneurial networks, and strategic guidance.

Embark exists to close this gap — structured learning, mentorship, and a supportive community that equips founders to build resilient, sustainable ventures.

We are sector-agnostic and welcome entrepreneurs from all industries — technology, agriculture, education, healthcare, fashion, manufacturing, the creative industries, retail, and beyond.

Our goals

  • Raise a new generation of African entrepreneurs
  • Encourage innovation and problem-solving
  • Promote job creation
  • Reduce poverty through entrepreneurship
  • Build financially independent young people
  • Inspire ethical leadership and lifelong learning
